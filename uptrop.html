Fundamental understanding of reactive nitrogen in the global upper troposphere

Reactive nitrogen is central to the influence of the upper troposphere on the global troposphere. Here we develop new and examine existing datasets to address persistent uncertainties in chemistry and sources of reactive nitrogen in the upper troposphere.
