The impact of rocket launches and space debris on the atmosphere and climate

The growth of the space sector is uncertain and air pollutant emissions are unregulated. Here we construct an inventory of emissions from rocket launches and burnup of returning space junk and implement these in the GEOS-Chem model coupled to a radiative transfer model to determine the impact on climate and the composition of the upper atmosphere.
